Let $\mathfrak{h}$ be a complex (pre-)Hilbert space. We define 

$$
\mathfrak{F}(\mathfrak{h})_{+} = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \mathfrak{h}^{n}_{+}
$$

to be the symmetrized Fock space over $\mathfrak{h}$.
